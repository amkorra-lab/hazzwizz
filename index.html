Skills

Some herritages will give you a bonus to Skills. Some of these are situational. Thus listed seperatly.

You gain 2 Ranks from your background and 2 Rank in one listed Lore/Profession.

Your chosen Lifestyle grants you 4 Ranks to distribute according to the skills your lifestyle offers you.

Additionally, you gain Skills from your Combat Focus (Usually 2 Skills at Rank 2, 6-12 Ranks +2x INT to distribute freely).
